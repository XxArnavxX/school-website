CISB and about it

CISB stands for Canadian International School Bangalore, it is a provate school and is certified by the Council of International Schools and New England Association of Schools and Colleges. It was founded in 1996, It only started with 6 students, and over the years it has grown, with approximetly 700 students from about 34 different contries. The school is considered to have an exceptional student-teacher ratio and the teachers represent about 27 nationalities Students follow the Cambridge Primary.
